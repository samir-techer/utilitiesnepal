Privacy Policy

We respect your data and believe in transparency.

🔒 Our Promise

Utilities Nepal is built with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Most tools work entirely within your browser using LocalStorage.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information. No accounts, no tracking pixels, no analytics cookies. The only data stored is what you voluntarily save in tools like Notes, which stays on your device via LocalStorage.

Local Storage

Notes, bookmarks, and preferences are stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party APIs

Some tools use public APIs (Weather, Currency, News). These services may collect IP addresses and request data as part of normal operation. We do not control their data practices.

Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. Only your theme preference (dark/light) is stored via localStorage for a consistent experience.

Contact

If you have privacy concerns, email us at utilitiesnepal@gmail.com.
